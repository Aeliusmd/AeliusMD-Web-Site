Employee health is one of the defining elements of a competitive business advantage.

At AeliusMD, we understand that employee health is one of the defining elements of a competitive business advantage, and we believe that occupational and environmental medicine is an investment in workforce productivity. AeliusMD offers the tools to assist companies and individuals in meeting their needs for occupational and environmental health and wellness over a broad time spectrum.

With over 30 years of experience in occupational medicine and workers’ compensation, the AeliusMD staff have extensive experience helping businesses reduce injuries, lower costs, and promote a safe workplace. We are dedicated to providing you and your staff with the highest quality software systems and unparalleled customer service.

On-site at your location, or at a state-of-the-art immediate care facility, we can meet all your occupational and industrial needs — including extended access to occupational and industrial medicine support staff with extensive experience with these patients.
