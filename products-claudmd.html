ClaudMD

ClaudMD is our AI-based EHR system, built to run the full occupational health case — from first visit to final report — in one connected record.

Features

Everything a case touches, in one system

01

Practice Management

Manage scheduling, staff workflows, and day-to-day operations from a single dashboard.

02

EHR System

A complete electronic health record purpose-built for occupational and industrial medicine.

03

Electronic Prescription

Send prescriptions electronically, with built-in checks to keep prescribing accurate and fast.

04

Referral Management

Track referrals in and out of your practice so no case falls through the cracks.

05

Order Tracking

Follow lab and diagnostic orders from request through result.

06

Document Management

Keep every case document — forms, imaging, correspondence — organized and searchable.

07

Billing System

Built-in billing tied directly to the record, reducing manual entry and errors.

08

Reporting System

Generate the operational and compliance reports your practice and clients need.
